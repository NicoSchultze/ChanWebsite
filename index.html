What I Do

Illustration of People

I focus mainly on depicting people, mostly women. I have found a deep satisfaction in displaying feminine facettes. As you can see in my gallery down below, I like to combine those female attributes with the ones of nature.

Illustration of Flora & Fauna

Next to my desire of displaying different kinds of people, I love displaying landscape as well as animals. My main illustrations are the visualisation of forests and plant life. Two of these paintings are seen below in the gallery.

Variety of Art

I am also very open to new ideas and still finding my place within art. I am trying out many different art styles and getting more and more into digital art. My spectrum of illustrations is very broad and not set to a specific niche.
